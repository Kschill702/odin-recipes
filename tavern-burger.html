Tavern Burger

Tavern burger (sloppy joe) is a easy and tasty meal everyone can enjoy!

Makes 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • Half cup ketchup
  • Quarter cup packed brown sugar
  • Quarter cup prepared yellow mustard
  • Hamburger buns

Cooking instructions

  1. Cook beef in medium skillet over medium-high heat until no longer pink, stirring to break up meat. Drain fat. Transfer beef to crock-pot
  2. Add ketchup, sugar and mustard to crock-pot, mix well. Cover; cook on low 4 to 6 hours. Serve on buns

Tip: Serve a scoopful on a hamburger bun. For added flavor, add a can of pork and beans when adding other ingrediants
