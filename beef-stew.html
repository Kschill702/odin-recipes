Easy Beef Stew

Delicious beef stew served on your choice of noodles

Makes 4 to 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 can (4 ounces) mushrooms
  • 1 envelope (1 ounce) dry onion soup mix
  • 1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
  • A third of a cup red or whited wine
  • Hot cooked noodles

Combine beef, mushrooms, soup mix, soup and wine in crock-pot. Cover; cook on low 8 to 12 hours. Serve over noodles, if desired

Tip: Browning the beef before cooking it in the crock-pot isn't necessary but helps to inhance flavor and appearence of the stew.
