French Onion Soup

Delicious meal, great for the whole family

Makes 8 servings

Ingredients

  • Half stick of butter
  • 3 pounds yellow onions, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons dry white wine or water
  • 2 quarts (8 cups) beef broth
  • 8 to 16 slices French bread
  • half cup shredded Gruyere or swiss cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Melt butter in larger skillet over medium-low heat. Add onions; cover and cook until onions are tender and transparent, but not browned, about ten minutes.
  2. Remove cover. Sprinkle sugar over onions. cook and stir 8 to 10 minutes or until onions are carmalized. Add onions and any browned bit to crock-pot. If desired, add wine to pan. Bring to a boil, scraping up any browned bits. Add to crock-pot and stir in broth. Cover and cook on low 8 hours or on high 6 hours
  3. Preheat boiler. to serve, ladle soup into individual soup bowls. Top each with 1 or 2 bread slices and 1 tablespoon of cheese. place under broiler until cheese is melted and bubbly
