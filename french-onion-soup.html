French Onion Soup

Delicious meal, great for the whole family

Makes 8 servings

Ingredients

  • Half stick of butter
  • 3 pounds yellow onions, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons dry white wine or water
  • 2 quarts (8 cups) beef broth
  • 8 to 16 slices French bread
  • half cup shredded Gruyere or swiss cheese
