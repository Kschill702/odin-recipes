Easy Chili

Delicious hot meal that's good year round (better when it's cold)

Makes 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cans condensed tomato soup, undiluted
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chili powder

Cooking instructions

  1. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef and onion. Cook and stir beef until beef is well browned. Drain exess fat
  2. Place meat mixture, soup and water in crock-pot. Add salt, pepper and chili powder to taste. Cover; cook on low 6 hours

Tip: This dish can be cooked up to 8 hours. Garnish with shredded cheese and serve with crackers or thick slices of Italian bread
