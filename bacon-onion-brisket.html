Bacon and Onion Brisket

Delicious slow cooked brisket topped with bacon and onions.

Makes 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bacon, cut crosswise into half inch strips
  • 1 flat-cut boneless brisket (about 2 and a half pounds)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 medium onions, sliced
  • 2 cans (10 and three quarter ounces each) condensed beef, undiluted

Cooking Steps

  1. Cook bacon strips in large skillet over medium-high heat about 3 minutes. DO NOT OVERCOOK! Transfer bacon with slotted spoon to crock-pot
  2. Season brisket with salt and pepper. Return skillet to medium-high heat and sear brisket in hot bacon fat on all sides, turning as it browns. Transfer to crock-pot
  3. Lower skillet heat to medium. Add sliced onions to skillet. Cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until softened. Add to crock-pot. Pour in condensed beef. cover; cook on high 6 to 8 hours or until meat is tender
  4. Transfer brisket to cutting board and let rest 10 minutes. slice brisket against the grain into thin slices, and arrange on platter. Add salt and pepper if desired. Spoon bacon, onions and cooking liquid over brisket and serve
