Bacon and Onion Brisket

Delicious slow cooked brisket topped with bacon and onions.

Makes 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bacon, cut crosswise into half inch strips
  • 1 flat-cut boneless brisket (about 2 and a half pounds)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 medium onions, sliced
  • 2 cans (10 and three quarter ounces each) condensed beef, undiluted
